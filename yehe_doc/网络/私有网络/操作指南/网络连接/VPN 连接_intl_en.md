[VPN connection](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1037/32679) is a transfer service based on network tunneling technology that brings about connectivity between local IDCs and resources on Tencent Cloud. It can help you to quickly build a secure and reliable encrypted tunnel on the Internet.
