Anti-DDoS allows you to block traffic from source IP addresses in specific geographic locations at the cleansing node, with just one click. You can block traffic from whatever regions or countries you need.
>?After you configure the regional blocking setting, attack traffic targeting the region will still be recorded but will not be allowed to your real server.

## Prerequisites
You have purchased an [Anti-DDoS Advanced instance](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/297/37241) and set the object to protect.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [Anti-DDoS Advanced Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/antiddos-advanced/package) and select **Anti-DDoS Advanced (New)** > **Configurations** on the left sidebar.
2. Select an Anti-DDoS Advanced instance ID in the list on the left, such as "xxx.xx.xx.xx bgpip-000003n2".
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/9c8bb19be4726a46a2261b35e048c8cb.png)
3. Click **Set** in the **Block by Location** section to get to configuration.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/c6f89965d075df77ccbf3302236260a5.png)
4. Click **Create**.
5. In the pop-up window, select a region to block and click **OK**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/f67ab4222a5822da34aa8cc8c09f72a8.png)
6. Now the new rule is added to the list. You can click **Configuration** on the right of the rule to modify it.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/80a4863f12ef9b977e55f2917012552e.png)
