


## Prerequisites
You need to [purchase an Anti-DDoS Pro instance](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1029/36115) and set the protected object first.

## Directions
1. Log in to the [new Anti-DDoS Pro Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ddos/antiddos-native/package) and click **Protection Configuration** on the left sidebar.
2. Select an Anti-DDoS Pro instance ID in the list on the left, such as "bgp-000000ij", and select **IP Port Protection** at the top on the right.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/6d820b131b435a50e4eb0b6ec74bdf08.png)
3. Click **Set** in the "AI Protection" block on the right to enter the AI protection list.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/84785dc3477e1f88b9eec01daaa0346f.png)
4. Click **Create** to create an AI protection rule and click the switch to enable AI.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/386e912652f281939038cf913cca5b3d.png)
5. After the creation is completed, an AI protection rule will be added to the AI protection list. You can modify the rule by clicking **Configure** in the "Operation" column on the right.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/3589bf2fd50b63ea2b693105b6ea5d8d.png)
