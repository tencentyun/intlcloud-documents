## Editing a Credential
1. Log in to the [SSM Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/ssm) and click **Database Credential** on the left sidebar.
 ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/af07530c506a0b6e98898c0834e14971.png)
2. Click the drop-down button in the top left corner of the credential list to modify the region.
     ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/5b39c941159d74d0b92476a9475a1c34.png)
3. In the search box on the right, enter the full or partial name of the credential you want to search.
     ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/d8dbfbdc69b5787cc38f143fc1827bd5.png)
4. On the right of the credential of your choice, click **Enable**/**Disable** to enable/disable it.
>?By clicking the credential name, you can change its status on the credential details page. For details, see [Editing a Database Credential](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1078/41802).
