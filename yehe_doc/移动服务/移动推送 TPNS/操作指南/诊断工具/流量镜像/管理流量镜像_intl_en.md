After a traffic mirror is created, you can enable, disable, modify or delete it or add a tag on the Console.
<span ID = "open"></span>

## Enabling or Disabling the Traffic Mirror
A new traffic mirror task is enabled by default. To disable it and then enable it again, follow the steps below.
1. Open the link in response to your [ticket submitted] (https://console.cloud.tencent.com/workorder/category) and log in to Traffic Mirror Console. In the top **Region** selector, choose the region where the traffic mirror is created.
2. Locate the traffic mirror you want to manage, disable or enable it under its  **Collect Traffic** column.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/4bb078b770da881c655e1568ab0ac5f4.png)

## Modifying the Traffic Mirror
To modify an existing traffic mirror, do the following:
1. Open the link in response to your [ticket submitted] (https://console.cloud.tencent.com/workorder/category) and log in to Traffic Mirror Console. In the top **Region** selector, choose the region where the traffic mirror is created.
2. Select the Name/ID of a traffic mirror you want to change.
3. Select the part you want to update and edit it as follows:
 - **Editing traffic collection**
    1. Click **Edit** on the top-right of the traffic collection.
    2. In the **Modify traffic collection configs** pop-up window, modify **Collection Range**, **Collection Type**, **Traffic filtering** or other configurations, and then click **OK**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/b0bec5e1875a500aefbd349706dd7492.png)
 - **Editing receiving IP**
    1. Click **Edit** on the top-right of the receiving IP.
    2. In the **Edit receiving IP** pop-up window, modify **Receiving IP** and **Balance method**, and then click **OK**.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/d63d19f5680390ec606d5f9efcaad2de.png)

## Adding a Tag
Tags are used to identify and organize Tencent Cloud resources. Each tag contains a tag key and a tag value. Adding a tag to the traffic mirror makes it easy to filter and manage traffic mirror resources.
1. Open the link in response to your [ticket submitted] (https://console.cloud.tencent.com/workorder/category) and log in to Traffic Mirror Console. In the top **Region** selector, choose the region where the traffic mirror is created.
2. Locate the traffic mirror you want to add a tag to, and click **Edit Tags** under its **Operation** column.
3. In the pop-up dialog box, do the following:
   1. For **Tag key**, enter the key name or select one in the drop-down list.
   2. For **Tag value**, enter the key value.
> A tag may have none or many key values.
>
   3. (Optional) To create multiple tags, click **Add**, and configure **Tag key** and **Tag value**.
   4. After completing the configuration, click **OK**.
4. Search for resources by tag.
   1. Click <img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/6ca6880fc850d1ec41695ec7c1714df7.png" style="width:18px;margin:-4px 0 ;"/> on the top right of the **Traffic mirroring** page, and select **Tag**.
      ![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/9c32dd53c4744ca5bd82403afb9f0b91.png)
   2. Enter a tag key in the field, and click <img src="https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/6ca6880fc850d1ec41695ec7c1714df7.png" style="width:18px;margin:-4px 0 ;"/>.
  > Separate tag keys with “|”.

## Deleting the Traffic Mirror
1. Open the link in response to your [ticket submitted] (https://console.cloud.tencent.com/workorder/category) and log in to Traffic Mirror Console. In the top **Region** selector, choose the region where the traffic mirror is created.
2. Locate the traffic mirror you want to delete, click **Delete** under its **Operation** column, and confirm the deletion.
