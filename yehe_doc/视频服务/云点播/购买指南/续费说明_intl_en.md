## Pay-As-You-Go Daily Billing Mode
If you use the [pay-as-you-go daily billing mode](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/266/14666), your VOD service will be billed according to your actual usage. To prevent your account from becoming overdue due to an insufficient balance, please top up your account in time in the console by following the steps below:
1. Log in to the Tencent Cloud console and click **[Billing Center](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/account/recharge)** in the top-right corner.
	![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/63ea19c83ff5f64c0c148297590e8d3f.png)
2. Select a payment method, enter the payment amount, and click **Pay Now**.
