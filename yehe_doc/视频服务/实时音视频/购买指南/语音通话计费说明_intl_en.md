## Usage Calculation

Your usage of the audio call service is the cumulative **audio duration** generated by all users in [TRTC rooms](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/647/37714#room).

- A user’s length of stay in a TRTC room is equivalent to the audio duration generated by the user.
- Billing starts the moment a user enters a TRTC room, even if he or she is the only user in the room.
- In cases where a user enters and leaves a room multiple times, TRTC will add up the durations of the user’s stays in the room.

>! A duration is calculated in seconds and monthly cumulative seconds are converted to minutes for billing. A duration of less than one minute is calculated as one minute.

About universal packages:
- The validity period of a universal package lasts from the day of purchase until the last day of the same month next year.
 For example, if you purchased a package on May 1, 2020, it would be valid from May 1, 2020 to May 31, 2021.
- You can purchase multiple packages. A duration is deducted in real time from the package that applies and expires the fastest.
- A new package takes effect in about 5 minutes after payment. **If your account has a duration that is generated after 00:00 of the day of purchase and hasn’t been deducted, it will be deducted immediately from the newly purchased package.
- To avoid interrupting your active businesses, **TRTC will not suspend its services for your account when your package is exhausted or expires**. The duration not deducted from your package will be billed under the [pay-as-you-go](#post-payment) mode.
- Once a universal package expires, the remaining minutes in the package will become invalid and cannot be retrieved.


<span id="post-payment"></span>
### Pay-as-you-go
When you overuse your package, the extra duration will be billed under the pay-as-you-go mode based on the [list price](#Fixed_price).
You can choose to pay [daily](#daily) or [monthly](#monthly).


<span id="daily"></span>
#### Pay-as-you-go daily

Accounts that create their first [application](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/647/37714) in the TRTC console on and after September 1, 2020 are charged daily by default under the pay-as-you-go mode. Fees for the day are deducted at 10:00 AM the next day.


<span id="monthly"></span>
#### Pay-as-you-go monthly
Accounts that created their first [application](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/647/37714) in the TRTC console on and before August 31, 2020 are charged monthly by default under the pay-as-you-go mode. Fees for the month are deducted within the first five days of the next month. For details, see [Bills](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/555/7432).

>!
>- You will be switched to the pay-as-you-go mode automatically after your prepaid package is exhausted or expires. You cannot switch to the mode by yourself.
>- You cannot change the billing cycle by yourself. If you wish to change the billing cycle from monthly to daily, please [submit a ticket](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/workorder/category).
>- If your account balance is insufficient for the deduction of the incurred fees, the overdue payment may cause other Tencent Cloud services to be suspended for your account too. For example, because on-cloud recording relies on **CSS** and **VOD**, your on-cloud recording task may fail due to the overdue payment.

## Billing Example
>! The example below uses the list price. You can [purchase a package](https://buy.cloud.tencent.com/trtc) to reduce your expenses.

Suppose user A, B, and C stayed in a TRTC room for 30 minutes, and none of them received video data throughout the process.
The **total cost** would be `the unit price of the audio duration x the total audio duration of all three users = 0.99 USD/1,000 min x (30 min + 30 min + 30 min) ÷ 1,000 = 0.0891 USD`.
