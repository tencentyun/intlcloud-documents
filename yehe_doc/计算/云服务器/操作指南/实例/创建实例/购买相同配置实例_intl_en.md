## Scenario
If you want to purchase the instance of the same configuration again, you can use the **Purchase with same configurationsâ€ feature on CVM Console, which saves you time and improves the scale-out efficiency under specific scenarios.

## Directions

1. Log in to the [CVM Console](https://console.cloud.tencent.com/cvm/instance/index?rid=19).
2. Select a region on the top of the **Instances** page.
3. Click **More** -> **Purchase with same configurations** under the **Operation** column of the instance.
![](https://main.qcloudimg.com/raw/990ca94e95a27a66b46a7ee5d20e296a.png)
4. Enter the quantity of CVMs you want to purchase, and confirm other configurations.
You may adjust the parameter configurations based on your actual needs.

5. Click **Purchase** and complete the payment.
