## Customer bill

### 1. View bills

>?
>- The billing time is about one hour. For a monthly subscribed product, its bill will be generated about one hour after the purchase. For a pay-as-you-go product, its bill will be generated about one hour after the product settlement cycle.
>- The bill of the current month may be delayed for real-time push network reasons. The displayed monthly bill shall prevail, which can be viewed on the third day of the next month.
>- Voucher deduction information is not displayed in the customer bills.
>- For products purchased offline, the product team will generate reseller bills and forward them to channel managers.
>- This page only displays the bills generated after the system upgrade. Please check your historical bills on the [historical bills](https://console.intl.cloud.tencent.com/expense/bill) page.

Step 1:Customer can log in to the [Console > Billing Center](https://console.intl.cloud.tencent.com/expense/customerbill)  to view your bill

![](https://qcloudimg.tencent-cloud.cn/raw/762a717f1891be0713dfb9a4af1adb03.png)

Step 3:In the filter area, you can filter bills by product, project, region, etc.
